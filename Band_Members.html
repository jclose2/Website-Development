Dave Adams

Lead Guitar

Dave Adams is the lead guitarist of Kwimby. Known for expressive solos, melodic riffs, and energetic live performances, Dave's playing has become one of the defining sounds of the band. Currently, due to the hiatus, Dave is the only official band member remaining currently.
