Quimby Becomes Kwimby

Published: March 2026

After years of performing under the name Quimby, the band has officially announced that it will now be known as Kwimby. The decision was made after discovering that the original name created confusion with other artists and businesses using similar names.

Rather than viewing the change as a setback, the band embraced the opportunity to establish a unique identity while keeping the same music and energy that fans have come to love. According to the band, "Only the spelling has changed—our passion for making music together remains exactly the same."

Fans have responded positively to the announcement, with many saying the new name better reflects the band's personality and gives them a distinct identity moving forward. Alternatively, they may also use the name "The Band Formerly Known As Quimby".

Kwimby Announces Hiatus

Published: June 2026

After many years of writing, recording, and performing together, the members of Kwimby have announced that they are taking an indefinite hiatus.

In a joint statement, the band explained that the decision came after ongoing creative differences regarding the future direction of their music. While discussions were respectful, the members ultimately agreed that taking time apart was the healthiest decision for everyone involved.

The band emphasized that this announcement is not being described as a permanent breakup. Instead, the members hope the time apart will allow everyone to pursue personal projects while leaving the possibility of future collaborations open.

"We're incredibly thankful for every fan who has supported us over the years," the statement read. "Although we're stepping away for now, we're proud of everything we've accomplished together and appreciate all of the memories we've shared."

The Kwimby fan community has responded with an outpouring of support and gratitude, celebrating the band's music while remaining hopeful that one day the members may reunite.
